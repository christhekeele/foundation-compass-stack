Foundation Sandbox Play nice.

This site displays every single Foundation element so that you can customize everything and take it in at a glance.

Fork it and use it as a starting point to develop your own customized Foundation installation. Then, import your stylesheets into your new website.

Or, customize it and use the Grunt, Compass, and Assemble stack it's built with as the starting point for your site.

Every component displays the full set of Foundation documentation to assist you in its customization.
