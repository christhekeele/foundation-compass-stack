Titleless Top Bars

If you want a bar that doesn't include a title, just take out the content within the list item, like so:

HTML
<nav class="top-bar" data-topbar role="navigation">
  <ul class="title-area">
    <li class="name"><!-- Leave this empty --></li>
    ...
  </ul>
</nav>
